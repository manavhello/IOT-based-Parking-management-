Parking System Rules & Regulations

This page outlines the rules and regulations governing the use of our system. By registering and using our services, you agree to be bound by these terms.

User Registration and Accounts

  • All users must be at least 18 years old and have a valid driver's license.
  • You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials.
  • Sharing your account details with unauthorized individuals is strictly prohibited.

Parking Allocation and Reservation

  • Parking spaces and time slots are allocated based on a first-come, first-served basis unless a reservation is made.
  • A maximum of one active reservation per user is allowed at a time.
  • Users are responsible for arriving within the reserved time slot. Late arrivals might forfeit their reservation.
  • Overstaying your allotted parking time will result in late fees and an extra 50rs charge with each hour.

Payment

  • All parking fees must be paid through the integrated UPI payment system before exiting the parking lot.
  • Failure to pay the due amount will result in enforcement actions, including potential vehicle immobilization.

Image Detection and Processing

  • The system utilizes image detection and processing for security purposes and to verify parking space allocation.
  • Users understand their vehicle image may be captured for identification and system operation.

General Conduct

  • Users are expected to park their vehicles within designated spaces and adhere to all posted traffic regulations.
  • Blocking other vehicles or obstructing traffic flow is strictly prohibited.
  • Damaging or tampering with system components is strictly prohibited.
  • The use of the system for any illegal or unlawful activity is strictly prohibited.

Additional Information

  • The system operators reserve the right to modify these rules and regulations at any time. Any changes will be communicated through the platform and signage within the parking area.
  • For any inquiries or concerns, please contact us through the provided channels listed on the Contact Us page.
  • By using this system, you acknowledge that you have read and understood these rules and regulations. We appreciate your cooperation in ensuring a smooth and efficient parking experience for everyone.
